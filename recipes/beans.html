Beans

Description

Beans is one of the most common dishes in Nigeria. Having relatively high carbohydrate and protein content, its a well-balanced meal.

And guess what? It's one of the simplest dishes to prepare ever. Follow along with me and make your very own delicious beans

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Beans
  • Onions
  • Two tablespoons of salt
  • One tablespoon of grinded pepper
  • Three cubes of maggi
  • One satchet of tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup of oil

Steps

  1. Select and sieve the beans
  2. Wash thoroughly and put it on the cooker
  3. Let it boil for about 5 minutes, then sieve the water out
  4. Put the water in it again and put it on the cooker
  5. Let it boil until the beans is soft
  6. Put in your onions, salt and pepper into it and stir
  7. Put your maggi into it and stir
  8. Put in the tomato paste and stir. Let it boil for a while
  9. Pour in the oil and stir thoroughly. Let it boil for like 2 minutes
  10. Turn off the cooker. Your beans is ready
