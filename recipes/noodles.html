Noodles

Description

S student life-saver and an extremely fast food to prepare.

We are going to be making noodles.

Ingredients

  • Satchet of noodles (as many as you want)
  • Onions
  • Green pepper
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Dice the onions, green pepper and carrots into bits and set them aside.
  2. Put about one cup of water on the cooker or more, depending on how many you plan to cook.
  3. Put the spices that come with the noodles in the boiling water. Put the onions and green pepper too.
  4. Put the noodles into the water. Let it boil for about 3 mins. Make sure to keep check the water level to prevent burning.
  5. Put in the carrots and stir.
  6. Let it boil for awhile. Turn off the cooker.
  7. Your noodles are ready!
