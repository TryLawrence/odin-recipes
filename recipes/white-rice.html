White rice

Description

We are going to be cooking the simplest form of rice there is: The white rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of Beans
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and put it on the cooker
  2. Put salt in it (optional)
  3. Let it boil until it's hot
  4. Sieve the remaining water out
  5. Your rice is ready
